  • Please aim to arrive 5-10 minutes before your class start time.
  • Latecomers may not be able to participate and will forfeit their session.
  • Please leave any shoes, bags and outdoor clothing in the cubby holes provided.
  • We do not take responsibility for your belongings within the car park or studio.
  • All lost property will be stored for 1 month and then taken to charity.
  • Please remember to bring a hair tie for long hair, remove all jewellery and bring a water bottle.
  • We are a zero-waste studio, please take any rubbish away with you.
  • We do not allow eating or drinking (other than water) within the studio.
  • If posting on social media, please ensure no one else is identifiable unless you have their permission.
  • Please tag Freedom of Flight Aerial in your posts.
  • We ask people to leave promptly and make sure they collect all belongings at the end of the session.
